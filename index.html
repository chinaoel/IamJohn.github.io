Project 2 : Would this song go viral?

We are curious what are some key factor that make music go viral


Therefore, we collected dataset from kaggle, combining with data from spotify API. The dataset spans from 1980 to 2023

We found the primary factor that affect the popularity of a song is its artist, and song duration.
